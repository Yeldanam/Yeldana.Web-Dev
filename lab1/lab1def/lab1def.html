Main Categories Overview

This page describes three important categories: Technology, Science, and Art. Each category has its own characteristics and role in modern society.

Technology

  • Improves communication
  • Automates processes
  • Supports innovation

Science

  • Explains natural phenomena
  • Uses experiments and research
  • Develops new knowledge

Art

  • Encourages creativity
  • Expresses emotions
  • Reflects culture
  • Inspires people
